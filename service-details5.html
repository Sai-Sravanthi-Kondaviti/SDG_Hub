Statistics

Gender inequality remains a significant global challenge, with 1 in 3 women experiencing physical or sexual violence during their lifetime.
Women occupy only 25% of seats in national parliaments, and over 200 million women and girls have undergone female genital mutilation (FGM).
In education, 130 million girls are still out of school, and women continue to face a 23% pay gap compared to men worldwide.
These statistics highlight the urgent need for action to promote gender equality and empower women and girls everywhere.

Goal 5 of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) focuses on achieving gender equality and empowering all women and girls. This goal addresses the systemic and structural inequalities that women and girls face globally in various sectors, including education, health, politics, and the workplace.
