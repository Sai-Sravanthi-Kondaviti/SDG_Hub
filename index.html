SDG INFORMATION HUB

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a set of 17 interconnected objectives established by the United Nations to address global challenges such as poverty, inequality, climate change, and environmental degradation, aiming for a more sustainable and equitable future by 2030. To monitor and report on progress toward these goals, various SDG information hubs have been developed, providing comprehensive data and statistics.

Key SDG Information Hubs:

  • United Nations SDG Global Database: Managed by the UN Statistics Division, this platform offers access to data on more than 210 SDG indicators for countries, areas, or regions worldwide.
  • SDG Transformation Center's Data Hub:An initiative of the UN Sustainable Development Solutions Network, this hub provides timely geospatial data and analytical reports on SDG progress, including country profiles and regional assessments.
  • SIDS Global Data Hub:Focused on Small Island Developing States (SIDS), this platform enhances institutional and technical capacities by offering access to relevant data sources, innovative dashboards, visualization tools, and integrated information systems.

Featured Goals

SDG-1

Aims to eradicate poverty in all its forms and ensure that everyone, especially those in vulnerable situations, has access to basic needs and opportunities for a better life.

SDG-2

Aims to end hunger, achieve food security, improve nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture for all people, particularly the most vulnerable.

SDG-3

Aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages by improving healthcare, reducing diseases, and addressing health inequities globally.

SDG-4

Aims to ensure inclusive, equitable, and quality education for all and promote lifelong learning opportunities to empower individuals and communities.

SDG-5

Aims to achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls by eliminating discrimination, violence, and unequal opportunities.

SDG-6

Aims to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all, promoting clean water access and improved hygiene practices worldwide.

SDG-7

Aims to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all, promoting the use of renewable energy sources and improving energy efficiency to support global economic development and environmental sustainability.

SDG-8

Aims to promote inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all, fostering innovation and improving living standards globally.

SDG-9

Aims to build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation to drive economic development and improve societal well-being.

SDG-10

Aims to reduce inequality within and among countries by promoting social, economic, and political inclusion, and ensuring equal opportunities for all.

SDG-11

Aims to make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable by improving urban planning and addressing challenges like pollution, housing, and infrastructure.

SDG-12

Aims to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns by promoting resource efficiency, reducing waste, and encouraging responsible production across industries.

SDG-13

Aims to take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts by enhancing resilience, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and promoting sustainable practices worldwide.

SDG-14

Aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development by addressing issues like pollution, overfishing, and ocean acidification.

SDG-15

Aims to protect, restore, and promote the sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, manage forests, combat desertification, halt biodiversity loss, and safeguard natural habitats.

SDG-16

Aims to promote peaceful, just, and inclusive societies by ensuring access to justice, building effective, accountable institutions, and reducing violence and corruption.

SDG-17

Aims to strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development through enhanced cooperation, financing, and data-sharing across countries and sectors.
