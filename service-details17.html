Statistics

SDG 17 focuses on strengthening global partnerships, but challenges remain in achieving its goals.
Official development assistance (ODA) fell short of the 0.7% target in many countries, with only 0.31% of GNI globally allocated to aid in 2020.
Investment in sustainable infrastructure and technology is growing, but developing countries still face financing gaps, particularly in climate adaptation.

Partnerships for the Goals emphasizes the importance of global cooperation and partnerships to achieve the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). It highlights the need for collaboration between governments, the private sector, civil society, and other stakeholders to mobilize resources, share knowledge, and ensure the successful implementation of the SDGs.

Top 5 key problem statements related to SDG 5: Partnership with Goals

  • Lack of centralised SDG information hub
  • Lack of Accessible Platforms for Global Collaboration
  • Insufficient Transparency and Accountability in Development Projects
  • Limited Access to Data and Research on Sustainable Development
  • Barriers to Financing and Investment for SDGs
