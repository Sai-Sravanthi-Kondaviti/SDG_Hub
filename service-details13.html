Statistics

Global temperatures have already risen by 1.1°C above pre-industrial levels, causing more frequent and intense extreme weather events.
Carbon dioxide (CO₂) emissions continue to rise, reaching 36.8 billion metric tons in 2022, with only 26% of global energy coming from renewables.
Climate-related disasters have affected over 3.6 billion people in the last decade, leading to economic losses

Climate Action aims to take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. This goal recognizes that climate change is a global challenge that requires immediate attention to mitigate its effects on the planet, ecosystems, and human societies. SDG 13 focuses on reducing greenhouse gas emissions, enhancing resilience to climate impacts, and promoting climate policies to address the growing challenges posed by climate change.
